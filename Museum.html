About Kaleidoscope

Our museum is a one-of-a-kind center where we invite people from all over the world to learn about and celebrate the accomplishments of people of color in the business field. Our museum consists of five floors, each with a different theme. The first floor consists of the lobby, the gift shop, and an IMAX theater. The second floor features Asian American and Pacific Islander people in the business field. The third floor features black people in business, and the fourth floor features Hispanic people in business. Finally, the fifth floor features Indigenous people in business. On each floor, there are staff members who lead activities for people of all ages. These include designing posters for marketing purposes and testing out products created by the entrepreneurs featured in the museum. There are also IMAX shows running from 11:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. on the stories of the people honored in the museum. Visitors have the opportunity to come to our museum and choose which groups of people they want to learn more about. In short, our museum allows for different ethnic groups to be honored and celebrated for their accomplishments in the business field.
