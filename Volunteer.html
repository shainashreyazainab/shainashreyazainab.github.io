Volunteers must be high school students or older, giving preference to individuals residing in the Tri-State area.
Volunteer (Usher)

Want to welcome the guests to the gala? If the answer is yes, sign up to be an usher! You will be asked to greet the guests, confirm their tickets, and perhaps receive payments at the door.

Volunteer (Server)

Would you like to serve food at the gala? This would require you to stand at the buffet-style stations and serve the food to the gala attendees who come to the stations. Volunteer hours will be provided upon request.

Volunteer (Set-up)

Ready to kickstart the event? If so, sign up to set up booths, tables, chairs, and sound system equipment (for performances) to make sure that everything runs smoothly during the gala.

Volunteer (Clean-up)

Have a knack for cleaning? If you do, what are you waiting for? Sign up to clean up after the gala. This includes wiping down tables, putting tables and chairs away, taking out the trash, and unplugging and packing up the sound system equipment. In other words, make the place look as spotless as it was before the event!
